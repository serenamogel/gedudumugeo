Best BBQ Sauce, According to Our Taste Tests [Updated!]

The best BBQ sauce is flavorful. That we know for sure. But what flavor should it be full of? That’s where things get complicated. BBQ sauce can mean so many things to so many different people and cultures. It can be sticky-sweet and thick. It can be thin and mustardy. It can be spicy. It can be fruity. The best barbecue sauce can be a dipping sauce, marinade, or sauce for braising. People drizzle it on artisanal pizzas and chopped salads. Want to get wild? Mix it with mayo for a sandwich spread. When it comes to the best BBQ sauce, there are basically limitless possibilities.

Over the course of multiple taste tests, we tried a wide variety of BBQ sauces to find the best BBQ sauces you can buy at the grocery store. So many of the sauces we tried were delicious, but these are the best barbecue sauces that you should run out and buy immediately. Whether you’re looking for the best BBQ sauce for ribs, the best BBQ sauce for pulled pork, the best spicy BBQ sauce, or even the best sugar free BBQ sauce , we have the best store bought BBQ sauce for you!

Best Sugar Free

G Hughes Sugar Free BBQ Sauce Hickory FlavoredWalmart

I searched and searched for a really good sugar free BBQ sauce to include in this best BBQ sauce ranking update. I tasted a lot of them. And they all tasted like artificial sweeteners. This pick from G Hughes (maker of many, many sugar free BBQ sauces) is no exception. It also tastes like artificial sweeteners—but not nearly as much as the others. It’s smoky. It has a little of that cola flavor you get from some classic BBQ sauces. It’s pretty darn sweet because of the artificial sweetener, but paired with some salty meats or fries, it’ll balance out. It’s not the best store bought BBQ sauce, but it is the best sugar free BBQ sauce we tried. — Justine Sterling

Best Low Sugar Carolina

Primal Kitchen Unsweetened Golden BBQ SaucePrimal Kitchen

Made with just one gram of sugar and no artificial sweeteners, this BBQ sauce from keto- and Whole30-friendly brand Primal Kitchen is, understandably, extremely savory. This is not a traditional sweet BBQ sauce. This is a tangy, mustardy, vinegary BBQ sauce with a touch of heat and smoke. If that’s your style, then this is the best (almost) sugar free BBQ sauce around. It’s also some of the best BBQ sauce for pulled pork if you like that peppery, tart, Carolina-style barbecue. I’m not always the biggest fan of Primal Kitchen’s products, but this one was a standout. — Justine Sterling

Best from Trader Joe’s

Trader Joe’s Carolina Gold BBQ Sauce

Not everyone was into Trader Joe’s Carolina Gold BBQ Sauce, but I loved it. Yes, it may not be BBQ-y enough by some people’s standards because it isn’t a reddish-brown color or made with tomato. I, on the other hand, love a mustard-based BBQ sauce. If you haven’t had it, think peppery honey mustard. It’s so tangy and not too sweet. The flavor perfectly contrasts fatty meat, creamy slaw, and soft bread, making it the best barbecue sauce for a BBQ meat sandwich. It’s definitely the best store bought barbecue sauce if you regularly shop at Trader Joe’s. — Jordan Myrick

Best Budget

365 Original BBQ SauceAmazon

Whole Foods’ 365 Original BBQ Sauce reminds me of fast food BBQ sauce in the best way possible. It’s mostly sweet but also quite salty and a little smoky. It’s not as complex as some of the other sauces on our list of the best BBQ sauces, but I like its simplicity. The texture is gelatinous, which makes it the best store bought BBQ sauce for dipping or coating anything. And, just like a fast food BBQ sauce, it’s cheap! Never pay McDonald’s 25 cents per sauce ever again. — Jordan Myrick

Best for Chicken Wings

Jack Daniel’s Old No. 7 Original BBQ SauceWalmart

I was shocked by Jack Daniel’s Old No. 7 Original BBQ Sauce. I don’t love alcohol in food because I typically find that booze completely overwhelms every other flavor. But Jack Daniel’s proves that alcohol in food can actually be great. This sauce is thick and sweet, with smoke and undertones of tang from the whiskey. Best Carolina Style

Lillie’s Q Carolina Barbecue SauceLillie’s Q

There isn’t just one kind of Carolina BBQ sauce. There’s the tangy, vinegary, Eastern North Carolina BBQ sauce. And there’s the sweet, tomatoey Western North Carolina BBQ sauce. This Carolina BBQ sauce from Lillie’s Q is the latter. It’s very, very tomatoey, with lots of black pepper flavor. It almost tastes like a Bloody Mary that decided on a career change. It’s totally different from any other store bought BBQ sauce on this list. If you’re missing that tomato-based sauce from back in sweet home North Cackalacky, this is the best Carolina BBQ sauce for you. — Justine Sterling

Buffalo Wild Wings Honey BBQWalmart

Want to bring that classic BW3 experience into the comfort of your own home? This is the best BBQ sauce the brand makes. It tastes like really good fast food BBQ sauce—sweet and thick, with noticeable honey flavor. And it even has a hit of heat. How much heat? Well, if you regularly douse your food in hot sauce, then this is a mild BBQ sauce. But if you use spice sparingly, then this will taste pretty hot.
